# AI-Driven Call Intelligence & Next-Best-Action  
## Problem Statement Document

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Modern sales and support organizations handle hundreds to thousands of calls daily. These calls contain critical business intelligence: customer objections, buying signals, dissatisfaction triggers, churn risks, competitor mentions, urgency markers, and hidden upsell opportunities.

However, this intelligence is largely trapped inside audio recordings.

Most organizations:
- Store calls for compliance
- Occasionally review calls manually
- Rarely extract structured insights
- Almost never automate follow-up decisions

As a result:
- Follow-ups are delayed
- Opportunities are lost
- Leads go cold
- Support escalations are reactive instead of proactive
- Managers lack visibility into call quality and patterns

There is no centralized intelligence layer converting call data into actionable business decisions.

This product aims to solve that gap.

---

# 2. Current State of the Industry

## 2.1 Manual Review Bottleneck

Managers cannot manually review:
- 100% of calls
- All objections
- All sentiment changes
- Every high-value conversation

Manual review:
- Is slow
- Is inconsistent
- Is subjective
- Does not scale

## 2.2 CRM Dependency on Manual Input

CRM systems rely on:
- Agents typing notes
- Agents remembering to log activities
- Agents choosing next steps manually

Problems:
- Incomplete notes
- Biased summaries
- Forgotten follow-ups
- Incorrect lead status updates

## 2.3 Lost Revenue Signals

Inside calls, customers often say:
- "Call me next week"
- "We are evaluating competitors"
- "Budget approval pending"
- "I need to check with my manager"

These statements indicate specific next actions.

But without structured extraction:
- They disappear into recordings.
